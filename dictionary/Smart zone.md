Early in a [session](./Session.md) the [agent](./Agent.md) is in a "smart zone" — sharp, focused, recall is good. As the session grows it drifts into a "dumb zone": sloppier, forgetful, more mistakes — and more **faithfulness [hallucinations](./Hallucination.md). Same [model](./Model.md), same [harness](./Harness.md) — just more [context](./Context.md). The felt effect of [attention degradation](./Attention%20degradation.md). [Clear](./Clearing.md) or [compact](./Compaction.md) when the session bloats; don't push through.

*Usage:* "It nailed the first three components and just butchered the fourth." "You're out of the smart zone — same model, just deep into the dumb zone now. Compact and reload the plan, the next component will land."
