An [agent](./Agent.md) spawned by another agent via a [tool call](./Tool%20call.md). Runs in its own [session](./Session.md) with its own [context window](./Context%20window.md), and reports a single [tool result](./Tool%20result.md) back. Distinct from a [handoff](./Handoff.md) — the parent specifically expects a return; a handoff has no return path. **Cannot spawn further subagents** — the tree is one level deep. Subagents exist to isolate context, not to compose hierarchies.

*Usage:* "The grep results are blowing out my context." "Spawn a subagent to do the search — it'll burn its own context window on the noise and report back the two file paths you actually need."
